📚 Learn About Waste Management

Understanding Chennai's waste segregation system

🌍 Why Waste Segregation Matters

Chennai generates over 6,500 tonnes of waste daily. Proper segregation helps:

  • Reduce landfill burden and environmental pollution
  • Enable efficient recycling and composting
  • Protect sanitation workers from hazardous materials
  • Create a cleaner, healthier Chennai

📋 Chennai Corporation Rules

As per Greater Chennai Corporation guidelines:

  • All households must segregate waste at source
  • Use separate bins for wet, dry, hazardous, and sanitary waste
  • Hand over segregated waste to collection workers
  • Penalties apply for non-compliance

♻️ Best Practices

  • Rinse containers: Clean plastic and glass before disposal
  • Flatten boxes: Compress cardboard to save space
  • Wrap hazardous waste: Seal batteries and medicines properly
  • Compost at home: Convert organic waste into fertilizer
  • Reduce & reuse: Minimize waste generation

Chennai's wet waste can be converted into compost within 45 days! By segregating properly, you're contributing to a circular economy and reducing the city's carbon footprint.
